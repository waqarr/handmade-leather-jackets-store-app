What is a Handmade Leather Jacket?

Leather jackets made by hand are designed and are made by humans - from beginning to end. Human resources do not seek assistance from machines that manufacture the garments, and the leather jacket is made with the help of a sewing machine. It's like stepping back to a day when production in mass was not feasible. Naturally, the machine which is in use nowadays is highly sophisticated and can do the job effectively. But the fact remains that a person makes handmade jackets without any outside assistance. Therefore the attention to detail is perfect.

The most crucial aspect of the information: hand-crafted leather jackets are specifically designed for the client and are designed and sized to satisfy the client's requirements. Every step is made with a focus on specific instructions. Thus, the cutting and stitching process is completed by hand and only for the customer. It takes longer to finish, but the result is unbeatable.

Correct Way to Take Measurements for a Bespoke Leather Jacket

It is essential not to make mistakes with your measurements, as it could alter your body size. And If that were an alternative, should you opt for a leather jacket, to begin with?


Therefore, you must follow the steps provided to measure the right way.

  • Find the measurement of the chest's width and length of your sleeves and shoulders from top to the top.
  • Do not forget to record your measurements for your back! Begin measuring from the top of your back to the point where you will end.
  • It's best to ask another person to measure you to ensure greater accuracy.
  • Make sure to record the dimensions in the form of a paper piece to have a reference when the time for placing your order arrives.
